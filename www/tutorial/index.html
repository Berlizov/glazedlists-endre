Glazed Lists Tutorial

A quick guide to get you up-and-running with Glazed Lists in your Java application.

Part 1 - Basics: displaying a simple table in a Frame.

Part 2 - Text Filtering: adding text filtering to your lists.

Part 3 - Custom Filtering: adding a custom filter to your lists.

Part 4 - Sorting: sort your lists faster than TableSorter.

Part 5 - Custom Renderers: display your data so it is easy to read.

Part 6 - Query Lists: use a dynamic backend for the lists.

Part 7 - JComboBox: replace the JTable widget with a ComboBox.

Part 8 - Performance Tuning: squeeze out more performance.

Tutorial Source Code

Download, review and test glazedlists-tutorial.zip. You will need glazedlists.jar on your classpath. For parts six and seven, you will also need your JDBC driver on your classpath.
